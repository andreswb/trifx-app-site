Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Quick Calculator?

Simply left-click on the triFX icon in your menu bar. A calculator window will open where you can convert any amount between currencies. Right-click opens the settings menu.

What do the rate change indicators mean?

The ‚Üë arrow means the rate increased since last update, ‚Üì means it decreased. No arrow means the change was less than 0.01%. This helps you spot trends at a glance.

Right-click on the triFX icon in your menu bar, select "Base Currency" and choose your preferred currency from the list. Your rates will update automatically.

How do I select which currencies to display?

Right-click the triFX icon, go to "Display Currencies" and select up to 3 currencies you want to track. Selected currencies will show checkmarks.

How often do exchange rates update?

Rates update automatically once per day when you start your Mac. You can also manually refresh rates anytime by right-clicking and selecting "Refresh Rates". If a fetch fails, triFX automatically retries with smart exponential backoff.

Green (üí±) means triFX successfully fetched real-time data. Red (üî¥) means the app is using cached data because the last refresh was unsuccessful. The üì° icon indicates no network connection.

Where does the exchange rate data come from?

Exchange rates are provided by ExchangeRate-API, a reliable financial data service. Rates are for informational purposes only.

Is my data private?

Yes! triFX stores all settings locally on your Mac. No data is sent to servers, and we don't track your usage. Previous rates are stored locally for change indicators.

Can I use triFX offline?

Yes, triFX caches the last successful exchange rates and previous rates for comparison, so you can view them and use the calculator even without an internet connection. The network status indicator will show when you're offline.

Why isn't my preferred currency available?

triFX supports 40+ major world currencies. If your currency isn't listed, it may not be available through our data provider.

How do I uninstall triFX?

Simply drag triFX from your Applications folder to the Trash. All settings and cached data will be removed.
