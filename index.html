Frequently Asked Questions

Click on the triFX icon in your menu bar, select "Base Currency" and choose your preferred currency from the list. Your rates will update automatically.

How do I select which currencies to display?

Click the triFX icon, go to "Display Currencies" and select up to 3 currencies you want to track. Selected currencies will show checkmarks.

How often do exchange rates update?

Rates update automatically once per day when you start your Mac. You can also manually refresh rates anytime by clicking "Refresh Rates" in the menu.

Green (üí±) means triFX successfully fetched real-time data. Red (üî¥) means the app is using cached data because the last refresh was unsuccessful.
